Don't Panic Team

What We Built

We created an immersive VR demo within Unity 3D that was rendered for output using the Google VR SDK, built for an iOS platform, and transferred to an iPhone from X-code. We coupled a spider animation that interacts with the user through auditory and visual cues to stimulate an emotional response, and within this same environment, built in a common trigger of PTSD- bombshells and gunshots from the war zone. Carefully timed audio and animation clips of an army of crawling spiders were coupled with audio clips heard during a war scene. Creepy spiders, gunshots, and further audio clips triggered by the user's movement all contribute to the ambience. An Apple watch with OS3 beta installed sends real-time heart rate (HR) data to the Healthkit, and a Unity plugin calls HR bpm values into our animation rendering. However, while we established connectivity with our code and could read the initial values, we were unable to maintain a reliable data stream during this limited time. Finally, of course, we made this website.
